My name is Mikhael, I am a Digital illustration designer

I am 26 years old, and I am from Georgia. Currently, I live in Italy, but my creativity started in Georgia, specifically in Tbilisi. During the student period. I studied at the Faculty of Visual Design at the Ilia Chavchavadze University of Georgia.

In my free time, I always listened to music, made sketches, and combined various drawings or ready-made objects, and then something interesting was created. But every time something was missing, it's like putting together a puzzle and in the process of completing it, you lose the central part of the puzzle.

In 2019, I started studying photography, music, and digital art simultaneously at the university. I wanted to learn a lot more about modern art!!! I could go into the depth of what is called details and find my style, and also the direction I needed. And every time I felt dissatisfied, I wanted to have something valuable and at the same time different from everyone else, and in 2020 I made this wish a reality.

My style is distinguished by radically different actions and directions. I create different illustrations only through the phone. With this, I emphasize the possibilities and presentation of my style.

I began my programming career at the age of 16, I was fascinated by coding while translating flowcharts programs into Python code. A little over a year ago I stumbled upon Blockchain technology, starting from Ethereum and then studying also other architectures. In my spare time I enjoy writing both technical and reflective articles on tech related topics.

Now I am a Digital illustration designer And I make Interior illustrations from various materials, and I offer them to the community who like art and illustrated views.

For me, art in a broad sense is an expression of human imagination and representation. Something different and not pre-exaggerated action. which combines reality and various forms of imagination.
